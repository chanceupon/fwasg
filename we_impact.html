Women Empowered Impact
Women Empowered Impact (WEimpact) aims to foster diversity of thought and promote improved personal and professional lives by empowering women for sustainable development.
To equip women in making life determining decisions by cultivating a process to create power in them for the betterment of their own lives, that of society and the community.in them for the betterment of their own lives, society and the community.
WEimpact themes
Financial Literacy
Empower women through promoting greater financial awareness and attitude, essential for financial well being and economic empowerment.
Health & Wellness
Promoting wellness in women with awareness on mental, physical, and emotional care across all life spans to create a healthy society and to be their best selves.
