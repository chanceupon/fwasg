FWA MENTORING Programme
The FWA Advancing Women in Finance (FWA‐AWF) Mentoring Programme has been running since 2011, and was awarded the AWARE New Initiative Award in 2013.
It is designed for mid‐career women in the banking and finance industry who seek guidance in professional and personal development from senior industry executives outside their organisations.
To date, we have successfully mentored 100 mentees, many of whom remain connected with their Mentors.
Our 2020 Programme enlisted the FWA Female and Male Champions as Mentors.

Overview of the FWA‐AWF Mentoring Programme
The FWA‐AWF Mentoring Programme has been running since 2011, and was awarded the AWARE New Initiative Award in 2013. To date, we have successfully mentored 100 Mentees, many of whom have let the professional relationship develop organically and stayed in touch with their Mentors.
In 2020, we received sponsorship from Seeds of Change Fund, supported by the Ministry of Social and Family Development and administered by the Singapore Council of Women’s Organisations. The funding has been used to develop the Programme manuals for the mentors and mentees, and other resource materials to support the mentoring journey.
We hope that both mentors and mentees find this a rewarding experience.
Goals:
To pair mid‐career female executives in the financial services industry with leaders and professionals with similar backgrounds/careers
Build a supportive community where mentors and mentees can openly discuss, share and learn from experiences
Increase awareness of FWA, and encourage mentees to contribute to FWA and its many agendas
This Programme lasts 6 months, and will include, after the successful of matching of Mentors and Mentees:
Six one‐on‐one Mentor‐Mentee sessions (arranged by mentees and mentors at their own convenience),
3 key events which cover skills development, self‐awareness exercises and networking opportunities for Mentors and Mentees

Mentee Commitments:
Organise a minimum of 6 meetings between Mentor and Mentee between the Programme
Attend a minimum of 70% of the Programme events (refer to Timeline)
Treat the information received during the Programme with strictest confidence and the utmost integrity
Mentee Eligibility:
Have more than 5 years' experience in the financial industry
Have a committed and enthusiastic attitude towards the Programme
Be willing to give back to FWA and community after graduating from Programme
Agree to partake in an interview process prior to admission
Upon a successful match with a Mentor, join FWA as an individual member (if you are not yet already a member)

Mentee and Mentor testimonials
Jane Tan, Mentee 2020
(Business Development Manager, VP, HSBC)
This mentorship journey is akin growing a plant with some pruning and multiple fertilising phases. I am grateful to have a career coach to help steer my thought process into the right direction and to provide the timely advisory when I had to do my first telephone and virtual interviews during this pandemic period.
This program also gives me the exposure to network with many dynamic, intelligent and authentic people who are more than willing to share their success stories with one another. It is my great pleasure to be part of the FWA community and in return, I hope to contribute meaningfully back to the community.
Gayle Chang, Mentee 2020
(Director, Head of APAC eDebt Sales, Global Markets, Deutsche Bank Singapore)
I have truly enjoyed the mentorship programme so far. It has been really well organized and carefully crafted to ensure maximum benefit to those joining. From the preselection rounds, kick off event, actual mentoring sessions to the mentee monthly sharing.
I have been able to connect to like minded women virtually, and I have managed to meet two in person.Would recommend it to anyone looking for support/guidance/growing their network in the broader finance community in Singapore.
Anubhuti Gupta, Mentor 2020 and Mentee 2012
