FWA Annual Symposium
FWA’s Annual Symposium is FWA’s annual flagship event that brings together FWA’s Champions in a single forum. FWA Champions unite in a single platform to share thoughts and solutions on issues where gender gaps still exist. This includes under presentation of females in senior corporate ranks, including on boards, gender pay gaps, financial security of women, access to capital for women, sharing personal responsibilities, mental and emotional wellness and the importance of females as role models and the role of mentors & sponsors. Our members get an exclusive opportunity to engage our Champions during the Symposium, network, and engage in discussions with fellow FWA members.
