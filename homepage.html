FWA Advancing Women in Finance Mentoring Programme 2020. Applications Now Open!
01 March 2020
The FWA Advancing Women in Finance (FWA-AWF) Mentoring Programme 2020 is now open for applications from 1 - 22 March 2020. Please click here to apply.
FWA has been running our award-winning mentoring programme called FWA Advancing Women in Finance (FWA-AWF) since 2011. We are returning this year with a refreshed approach!
Join our unique mentoring programme, intended to offer guidance for personal and professional growth, to mid-career women in the banking and finance industry.
Our mentors this year come from a prestigious group of Male and Female Champions of FWA. They are dedicated senior industry executives, committed to developing female leaders of tomorrow. This programme also offers the opportunity for you to meet and build relationships with like-minded peers.
Find out more here.
Mission statement
To promote education, advancement and recognition of women in the financial sector; not foregoing work-life balance.
To encourage young female leaders-of-tomorrow to seek career opportunities and advancement in finance through mentorship programs.
To provide a networking platform for professional support and contacts.
To contribute to social and community development by offering financial or resource support to charitable causes, with particular focus on women’s needs.
